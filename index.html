Questionnaire

Enter both player names and start. Spin to randomly choose who answers. The selected player answers the question. The other player decides to Accept (+1) or Challenge (+2). 15 rounds. Highest score wins.

Truth or Dare

Enter both names and spin the wheel. The wheel selects who must choose. They must pick Truth or Dare and complete it. No skipping. 15 rounds.
